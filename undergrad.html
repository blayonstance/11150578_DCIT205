Welcome to the Undergraduate Programs in Computer Science and Information Technology!

Explore the exciting world of technology through our innovative and dynamic undergraduate programs. At University Of Ghana, we are committed to providing a comprehensive and enriching learning experience.

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSc CS)

    Dive into the foundations of algorithm design, software development, and computational theory. Explore the exciting world of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cybersecurity.

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc IT)

    Gain expertise in network administration, database management, and IT project management. Learn to harness the power of technology to solve real-world problems.

    • Your Journey Starts Here

    Whether you're passionate about coding, intrigued by cybersecurity, or fascinated by the potential of data, our undergraduate programs offer a diverse range of opportunities to explore and specialize in your chosen field. Join us on a journey of discovery, innovation, and academic excellence.

MPhil and MSc Programs

Our MPhil and MSc programs in Computer Science are designed to provide advanced knowledge and skills in various areas of computing.

Admission Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Letters of recommendation.
  • Statement of purpose.

Curriculum Overview

The programs cover a wide range of topics, including:

  • Advanced algorithms and data structures.
  • Machine learning and artificial intelligence.
  • Database systems and data management.
  • Software engineering principles.

Opportunities for Specialization

Students have the option to specialize in areas such as:

  • Computer Vision
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Cybersecurity
  • Big Data Analytics

Thesis/Dissertation Requirements

Completion of a research-based thesis or dissertation is a key component of the programs.

APPLY
